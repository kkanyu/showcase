Character Showcase

In Genshin Impact, the highest rarity for a character is 5☆ units. Characters with 5☆ rarity are difficult to obtain from the gacha system, thus some players will spend exorbitant amounts of money in the game to make sure they can own their favourite characters. This is called "whaling."

As at the 2.4 update, Fleeting Colours in Flight, there are a total of 20 5☆ units in the game, excluding the free starter character, Traveller, and the free cross-over unit, Aloy. The following is a showcase of 5☆ characters owned by Kay. The collection also includes two unreleased units confirmed to be released by Patch 2.6.

The character showcase divides characters into groups based on region they come from: Mondstadt, Liyue Harbour, and Inazuma.

In Genshin Impact, when you pull duplicates of a character, you earn Stella Fortuna for the character. This allows you to activate their Constellations, unlocking new abilities. The maximum number of Constellations is 6. To have a character with maximum constellations, you must pull them 7 times. The base character is considered C0.
